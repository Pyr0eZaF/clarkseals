Industries We Serve

Clark Seals provides high-performance sealing solutions across a wide range of industries, ensuring reliability in even the toughest conditions. Our seals are trusted in heavy equipment, automotive, industrial machinery, agriculture, aerospace, and more, delivering superior protection and longevity. No matter the application, Clark Seals offers precision-engineered products and expert service to keep your operations running smoothly.

  • Aerospace

    Clark Seals provides high-performance seals that withstand extreme temperatures, pressure, and demanding flight conditions.

  • Agriculture/Construction

    Our durable seals protect heavy machinery from dirt, moisture, and harsh environments for longer equipment life.

  • Appliance

    We manufacture precision seals that enhance appliance performance, efficiency, and leak protection.

  • Automotive

    Clark Seals delivers advanced sealing solutions that improve vehicle reliability, efficiency, and durability.

  • Energy

    Our seals ensure reliable operation in oil, gas, and renewable energy applications under extreme conditions.

  • Industrial Equipment

    We provide heavy-duty seals that protect industrial machinery from contaminants, wear, and harsh environments.

  • Medical Equipment

    Our seals meet strict medical standards, ensuring safety, hygiene, and reliability in critical applications.

  • Outdoor Power

    Clark Seals protects outdoor power equipment from dirt, water, and wear for extended performance.

  • Process Industries

    We offer seals that resist high temperatures, pressure, and chemicals in manufacturing and processing.

  • Power Tools

    Our seals enhance power tool durability by preventing dust, vibration, and excessive wear.

  • Power Transmissions

    We provide precision seals that prevent leaks and reduce friction in gearboxes and motors.

  • Rail

    Clark Seals delivers rugged seals that withstand vibration, heavy loads, and extreme weather.
