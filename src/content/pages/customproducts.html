Products

Compression Molded Rubber Parts

Compression molded rubber parts are custom-shaped components formed by pressing uncured rubber into heated molds. Ideal for medium to large production runs, they offer excellent durability, precision, and material versatility for applications including seals, grommets, and isolators.

Machined Rubber Products

Machined rubber products are precision-cut from solid rubber using CNC or lathe equipment, allowing for tight tolerances and complex geometries. They are ideal for prototypes, low-volume production, and custom sealing applications requiring high accuracy and performance.

Extruded Rubber Products

Extruded rubber products are manufactured in continuous lengths with uniform cross-sections, such as tubing, seals, or trim profiles. Produced from a variety of elastomers, they offer a cost-effective solution for flexible, long-length applications requiring consistent shape and performance.

Rubber Bonded to Metal

Rubber bonded to metal components combine the elasticity of rubber with the strength of metal, creating durable, integrated parts for vibration isolation, shock absorption, and structural support. Commonly used in automotive and industrial applications, these components offer reliable performance in harsh environments.
