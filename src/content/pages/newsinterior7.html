November 15, 2022
Tulsa, OK

New Sales Office In Texas

Clark Seals, a leading designer and manufacturer of high- performance sealing solutions, is proud to announce the opening of a new sales office in Texas. This expansion marks a strategic move to better serve customers in the Southeastern United States and strengthen the company’s commitment to providing responsive, industry-leading support across the region.


The new Texas office offers dedicated sales and technical support, allowing Clark Seals to provide real- time assistance and faster response times to original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) in the area. This expansion is part of Clark Seals’ larger customer support initiative aimed at delivering unmatched service to manufacturers in agriculture, construction, automotive, energy, and other key industries.


“This new expansion provides customers in the Southeast with 24-hour assistance and is part of a larger customer support initiative to provide industry-leading support to manufacturers across various industries,” said Scott Zahn, President of Clark Seals. “By bringing our resources closer to our customers, we’re improving response times and strengthening relationships with the companies that count on Clark Seals for critical sealing applications.”


Clark Seals has built a reputation for excellence over nearly 70 years, with USA-based manufacturing, engineering, and inventory capabilities that consistently deliver the highest quality products and best-in- class service.


For more information, visit www.clarkseals.com or contact the Clark Seals team at info@clarkseals.com.
