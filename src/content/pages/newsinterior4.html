January 4, 2025
Tulsa, OK

WhirlPool’s Award Press Release

Clark Seals, a trusted supplier of precision-engineered sealing solutions, is proud to announce that it has earned a Perfect Supplier Scorecard Rating for 2024 from Whirlpool Corporation—one of the world’s leading manufacturers of home appliances.


This recognition highlights Clark Seals’ exceptional performance across key metrics including quality, on-time delivery, cost competitiveness, and customer service. Achieving a perfect score reflects the company’s ongoing commitment to operational excellence, continuous improvement, and dependable support for global OEMs.


“Whirlpool sets a high bar for supplier performance, and we’re honored to meet and exceed those expectations,” said Scott Zahn, President of Clark Seals. “Earning a perfect scorecard rating is a reflection of our entire team’s dedication to delivering consistent quality and service. We value our partnership with Whirlpool and look forward to building on this success.”


Clark Seals has been a long-standing partner to manufacturers across industries, delivering USA-based engineering, quality control, and inventory support for over six decades. The company’s partnership with Whirlpool underscores its ability to meet the strict requirements of top-tier manufacturers while maintaining flexibility and responsiveness in a dynamic global market.


For more information, visit www.clarkseals.com or contact customerservice@clarkseals.com.
