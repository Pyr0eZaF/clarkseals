Gaskets

Gaskets create a tight, leak-proof seal between flat mating surfaces, preventing the escape of fluids or gases in static applications such as flanges, housings, and enclosures. Available in a wide range of materials—such as rubber, PTFE, graphite, and metal—gaskets can be tailored for compatibility with temperature extremes, chemical exposure, and pressure demands. Gaskets can be precision-cut or molded to fit complex shapes and tight tolerances, ensuring an exact fit and optimal sealing performance in specialized applications.

Key Features:

  • Reliable Static Sealing
  • Material Versatility
  • Customizable for Complex Geometries
