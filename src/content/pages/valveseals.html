Valve Seals

Valve seals regulate the amount of oil that lubricates the valve stem, preventing excess oil from entering the combustion chamber while ensuring adequate lubrication for reduced wear. Made from advanced elastomers or PTFE blends, valve seals withstand extreme engine temperatures and exposure to fuels, oils, and combustion byproducts without degrading. Engineered for tight tolerances, valve seals maintain consistent contact with the valve stem and guide, ensuring long-term performance, reduced emissions, and improved engine efficiency.

Key Features:

  • Controlled Oil Management
  • High-Temperature and Chemical Resistance
  • Precision Fit and Long Service Life
