Piston Seals

A hydraulic seal is a specialized sealing component used to contain hydraulic fluid and prevent leakage in high-pressure hydraulic systems. Designed to operate in cylinders and pistons, hydraulic seals maintain pressure, direct fluid flow, and protect internal components from contamination. They are typically made from durable materials like polyurethane, rubber, or PTFE and are engineered to withstand extreme pressures, temperatures, and dynamic motion. Hydraulic seals are essential for the efficient, safe, and reliable operation of equipment such as loaders, presses, and industrial machinery.
