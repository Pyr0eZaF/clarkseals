Julya Humphrey Selected as Accounting Controller

Clark Seals announced the appointment of Julya Humphrey as Accounting Controller. In this role, she will enhance accounting processes, streamline efficiencies, and strengthen the company's financial operations.

Clark Seals announced the appointment of Julya Humphrey as Accounting Controller. In this role, she will enhance accounting processes, streamline efficiencies, and strengthen the company's financial operations.

"Julya brings a wealth of accounting experience during a time of significant growth," commented Scott Zahn, Director of Sales. "She is a valuable addition to our management team, and I look forward to her leadership as we expand operations in North America and Asia."
