JUNE 2nd, 2025
Tulsa, OK

10-year on-time delivery achievement

Clark Seals, a U.S. manufacturer of precision sealing solutions, is proud to announce a major milestone in supply chain performance: 99.7% on-time delivery maintained over the past 10 years. This achievement underscores the company’s unwavering commitment to reliability, operational excellence, and customer satisfaction.


In an era of global supply chain disruptions, Clark Seals has distinguished itself as a dependable partner to OEMs across industries including agriculture, construction, automotive, energy, and appliances. The company’s consistent delivery performance is driven by its U.S.-based manufacturing, strategic inventory programs, and a customer-first culture rooted in accountability and precision.


“Our customers depend on us to keep their production lines moving, and we take that responsibility seriously,” said Scott Zahn, President of Clark Seals. “To maintain 99.7% on-time delivery over a full decade is not just a statistic—it’s a reflection of the systems, people, and values that define who we are.”


Clark Seals has invested heavily in domestic capacity, automation, and logistics optimization to ensure stable supply amid shifting global conditions. The result: uninterrupted service, minimized downtime for customers, and a performance record that stands among the best in the industry.


For more information, visit www.clarkseals.com or contact info@clarkseals.com.
