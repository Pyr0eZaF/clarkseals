End Caps

End caps are designed to seal the open end of a housing or bore, preventing the ingress of contaminants and the escape of lubricants in static sealing applications. Typically installed with a secure interference fit, end caps provide a reliable seal without the need for additional fasteners or adhesives, simplifying assembly and maintenance. Made from robust materials such as rubber-coated metal or high-performance polymers, end caps withstand exposure to dirt, moisture, and varying temperatures, making them ideal for use in gearboxes, motors, and housings.

Key Features:

  • Effective Shaft Bore Closure
  • Durable, Press-Fit Design
  • Resistant to Environmental Exposure
