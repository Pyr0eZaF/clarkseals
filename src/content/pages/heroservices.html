Our Services

Our Service

Custon Seal Desgin

At Clark Seals, we specialize in custom seal design, whether you need a brand-new solution or modifications to an existing one. Our expert team engineers durable, high-performance seals tailored to withstand extreme conditions, from high-speed applications to intense temperatures and pressures. We work closely with you to ensure every seal meets your specific needs, delivering precision-engineered solutions that enhance efficiency and reliability across industries.

Our Service

Application Engineering

Clark Seals provides full engineering support to optimize seal performance and extend equipment life. Our team collaborates with engineers to refine design elements, material selection, and key performance factors like lubricant compatibility, surface finish, and tolerances. Whether for new machinery or existing equipment, we deliver precision-engineered sealing solutions that minimize wear, reduce failure rates, and ensure long-term efficiency in even the most demanding applications.

Our Service

Problem Seal Analaysis

Clark Seals prioritizes fast and effective problem resolution to minimize downtime and equipment damage. Our engineering team can diagnose and redesign most failed seals within 24 hours by analyzing key variables and identifying the root cause. Using our proprietary optimization protocol, we develop custom AutoCAD designs to ensure long-term reliability and peak performance. No matter the issue, we provide precise, lasting solutions to keep your operations running smoothly.

Our Service

Precision Manufacturing

Clark Seals Ningbo (CSN) is a world-class seal manufacturing facility, combining advanced technology with industry expertise to produce high-performance sealing solutions. Since 2012, CSN has maintained the highest standards through precision engineering, a rigorous 12-stage quality control process, and an elite team of engineers. As an American-owned, ISO and TS-certified facility with a 40-million-piece annual capacity, we ensure every seal meets exacting durability and performance standards. With a commitment to excellence and a debt-free operational model, Clark Seals delivers unmatched reliability in precision manufacturing.
