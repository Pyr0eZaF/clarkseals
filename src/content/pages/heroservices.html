Our service

Application Engineering

At Clark Seals, we provide full engineering support for both new and existing applications, ensuring that your seals perform at their best and extend the life of your equipment. Our expert team works closely with equipment engineers to optimize every aspect of seal performance, from material selection to design refinements, helping to minimize wear, reduce failure rates, and enhance overall efficiency.

As a dedicated seal engineering firm, we analyze critical factors such as lubricant compatibility, material properties, fluid levels, shaft and housing hardness, surface finish, tolerances, chamfers, and eccentricity to create the ideal sealing solution for your specific application. Whether you're developing new machinery or improving existing equipment, Clark Seals delivers precision-engineered solutions designed to meet the toughest performance demands. Let us help you achieve optimal sealing conditions and maximize your equipment’s longevity.

Our service

Problem Seal Analaysis

Seal failures can lead to costly downtime and equipment damage, which is why Clark Seals prioritizes fast and effective problem resolution. Most failed seals can be diagnosed and redesigned within 24 hours, minimizing disruptions to your operations. Our expert engineering team quickly analyzes the failed seal, evaluates key equipment and operating variables, and pinpoints the exact cause of failure.

Using our proprietary seal optimization protocol, we develop a custom design in AutoCAD and provide a detailed review for your approval. Whether the issue stems from material degradation, improper fit, or environmental conditions, we tailor a solution to ensure long-term reliability and enhanced performance. At Clark Seals, solving seal failures is our top priority—we are committed to delivering fast, precise, and lasting solutions to keep your equipment running at peak efficiency.

Precision Manufacturing

Clark Seals Ningbo (CSN) is one of the most advanced seal manufacturing facilities in the world, combining cutting-edge technology with industry-leading expertise to produce high-performance sealing solutions. Since its completion in 2012, CSN has been dedicated to precision engineering, utilizing state-of-the-art manufacturing and testing equipment, a rigorous 12-stage inspection and quality control process, and an elite team of seal engineers from around the globe.

As an American-owned, ISO and TS-certified facility, CSN upholds the highest standards of quality and efficiency. With a production capacity of 40 million pieces annually and a team of 130 skilled employees, including 10 dedicated seal engineers, we ensure that every seal meets exacting performance and durability standards. Our commitment to excellence, along with a debt-free operational model, allows us to provide superior products with unmatched reliability. At Clark Seals, precision manufacturing isn’t just a process—it’s the foundation of our promise to deliver the best sealing solutions in the industry.
