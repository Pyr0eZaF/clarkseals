Radial Shaft Seals

Shaft Seals, also known as a rotary shaft seals, are mechanical components used to prevent the leakage of fluids (such as oil, grease, or water) along a rotating shaft. They also keep contaminants like dirt and moisture out of the machinery. Clark Seals designs optimized shaft seals that are perfectly matched to each individual application, ensuring maximum reliability and performance.
