AUGUST 8th, 2022
Tulsa, OK

New Texas Engineering Center

Clark Seals has officially opened a new Engineering and Technical Support Center in Texas, further expanding its footprint and technical capabilities in the United States. This dedicated facility is designed to accelerate product development, enhance customer collaboration, and support the growing demand for advanced sealing solutions across multiple industries.


Strategically located to serve OEMs in the southern and southeastern U.S., the new center will focus on application-specific engineering support for manufacturers in appliance, agriculture, automotive, energy, industrial equipment, and others.


“This new engineering center is a major step forward in how we partner with our customers,” said Scott Zahn, President of Clark Seals. “It gives engineers and OEMs a direct connection to our design and materials experts, and allows us to move faster from concept to solution.”


The Texas Engineering Center is part of Clark Seals’ long-term strategy to invest in U.S.-based innovation, reduce development cycles, and deliver engineered sealing solutions that meet today’s performance, durability, and regulatory standards.


With over six decades of experience and a proven record of on-time delivery, quality, and service, Clark Seals continues to lead with customer-focused investments that bring value beyond the product.
