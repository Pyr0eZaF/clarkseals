March 11th, 2011

Scott Zahn Joins as Director of Sales

Clark Seals announced that Scott Zahn has joined the company as Director of Sales. In this role, he will oversee all domestic sales divisions and manage the sales representative network.

Previously, Zahn served as Vice President of Retail Sales for Winston Company—expanding mass retail brands and deploying new product initiatives—and as President of Global Bearings, Inc. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from the University of Tulsa.
