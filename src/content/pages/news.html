Clark Seals Hires Jeff Thompson as Sales Manager

TULSA, OK, December 1, 2022

Clark Seals, a sealing technology company, announced today that Jeff Thompson has joined the company as Territory Sales Manager. In his new position, Thompson will manage sales operations in Texas, Oklahoma, Arkansas, Louisiana, Mississippi and Alabama.

Prior to joining Clark Seals, Thompson served as Industrial Manager at Parker Hannifin Rotary Seal Division where he successfully expanded sales and strengthened customer relationships. Before that, he served as Territory Sales Manager at JM Clipper and holds a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration from Letourneau University.

"Jeff brings a wealth of industry knowledge and professionalism to our organization and will play an important role in our company's sales initiatives," commented Scott Zahn, Director of Business Development. "With more than 20 years of experience in the sealing industry and uncompromising integrity, I believe Jeff will quickly become a valued resource for manufacturers looking to improve supply of rubber and sealing products."

About Clark Seals: Founded in 1956, Clark Seals is a leader in elastomeric seal design and manufacturing for the Agriculture, Appliance, Automotive, Construction, Energy, Industrial, and Outdoor Power equipment industries. Headquartered in Tulsa, Oklahoma, the company also has sales and engineering offices across several U.S. states.

Clark Seals Hires Jesse Slagell as Director of Engineering

TULSA, OK, August 26, 2022

Clark Seals, a fluid sealing technology company, announced that Jesse Slagell has joined as Director of Engineering to oversee the company's engineering operations and initiatives.

Prior to joining, Slagell served as Senior Product Engineer for Parker Hannifin where he designed custom rotary seals for various applications including Automotive, Aerospace, Military, and Industrial. He holds degrees in Mechanical Engineering, Mathematics, and Applied Science from LeTourneau University.

"Jesse brings a wealth of engineering expertise to our company," commented Scott Zahn, Director of Business Development. "His commitment to building a world-class seal engineering program will strengthen our products and services and play an important role in our company's future development."

Completion of $20M Shaft Seal Factory in Ningbo, China

TULSA, OK, December 23, 2012

Clark Seals announced the completion of its state-of-the-art shaft seal manufacturing facility in Ningbo, China. The ISO/TS certified facility features advanced automation, manufacturing, and testing equipment along with stringent quality control measures. With a capacity of 40 million pieces, Clark Seals Ningbo (CSN) is ready to meet high-volume OEM requirements.

"Clark Seals Ningbo is the first high-end shaft seal factory in China," commented Scott Zahn, Vice President of Operations. "CSN manufactures the same quality and consistency as the best Japanese and German factories, at a significantly reduced price, offering manufacturers in the US, Asia, and Europe a tremendous opportunity to improve quality and reduce costs."

Following successful factory audits, two leading appliance manufacturers have transitioned their entire seal production to CSN.
